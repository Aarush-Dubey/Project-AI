How to Play

Use the arrow buttons or keyboard arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Or let the AI play for you:

  • Random Move: AI makes a single random move
  • Greedy Move: AI makes a single move that maximizes immediate score
  • Auto Play: AI plays automatically until game over
